Introduction to Dead-Tree Video Gaem

Dungeons & Dragons is a video game from the olden times. Before humans evolved from apes in the late 1980s and invented the personal computer, they would congregate in meatspace and simulate a video game using only pens, papers, dice, and some a mythical force called 'imagination'.

How do I play

Create a Character

You nerds are all used to character creation being an unimportant aspect of the vidya gaem — you pick a class, move some face sliders, and you're good to go.

IT'S NOT SO EASY HERE.

Beyond physical apperance, class, and stats, you need to come up with a backstory. Some questions to ask yourself:

  • What is my character doing with their life? Why are they a warrior/monk/tower hugging necro?
  • What does my character want to accomplish? Revenge on an enemy? Become a famous explorer? Invent a new magical weapon?
  • What are the character's flaws? Are they a raging alcoholic? Do they never back down, even when they're wrong and their healthcare bill will kill millions of Americans?

Roll20 VTT

Combat in DnD is basically impossible to do in your head; it involves positioning, ranges, tracking a bunch of numbers, and all kinds of obnoxious bullshit that computers could do instead.

So, since we have computers, we will be using the Roll20.net virtual table top to manage the game. This includes a grid in which we can put pictures, digital dice, and a bunch of other collaboration tools.


What are we playing?

We will use the DnD 5E rules. They're relatively simple — most things are decided by rolling a d20 and adding a modifier, and there's a wealth of pre-made modules and tools we can take advantage of.

However, we still need to decide on our campaign setting.

Forgotten Realms
Tolkien-esque fantasy setting with swords and sorcerty. There world has lots of gods and large-scale magical crisises.
Eberron
A steampunk setting. Magic is basically used as electricity in this setting, and heavy magical shit (rez spells & other big things) are missing. We do get airships, chainswords, and a big focus in jamming magic into gear to do cool shit.
